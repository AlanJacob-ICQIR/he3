What is Helium-3

The fuel of the future
is sitting on the Moon

Helium-3 is a rare isotope that barely exists on Earth but is abundant on the lunar surface. Scientists have long identified it as an ideal fuel for nuclear fusion — a clean, efficient energy source with no radioactive waste. At roughly $20,000 per gram, it is one of the most valuable substances known to science.

☀️

Clean Fusion Fuel

He-3 enables aneutronic fusion reactions — producing power with no radioactive byproducts. It is the most efficient fuel candidate for next-generation fusion reactors.

🌕

Lunar Abundance

The solar wind has deposited He-3 onto the Moon's surface for billions of years. NASA, SpaceX, and China's space program are all actively pursuing lunar resource extraction.

💎

Extreme Scarcity on Earth

Earth's atmosphere contains barely any He-3. Current global supply is measured in kilograms per year — nowhere near enough to power a fusion economy.

📈

No Liquid Market Existed

Until HE3, there was no way to gain financial exposure to Helium-3. No futures, no ETF, no index. HE3 is the first and only instrument that tracks He-3 price sentiment.

How to Buy

four steps
